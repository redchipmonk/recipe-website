Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description

This simple recipe of baked mac and cheese will leave you and the people you are serving wowed and craving more. These people will be begging you to make more and you'll have to oblige because you are that guy/gal plus it's so easy!

Ingredients

  • 1 package elbow macaroni
  • 1 lb shredded cheese
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon of your choice of seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit and spray/grease a baking dish.
  2. Cook pasta as directed on the package.
  3. While the pasta boils, combine 2 and 1/2 cups of cheese with flour and seasoning.
  4. When pasta is cooked and drained, mix with cheese mixture.
  5. Place in greased pan, pour milk over macaroni. Top with remaining cheese and dot with buttter. Cover with foil.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes, taking foil off after 30 minutes for crispy top.
  7. Cool for 10 minutes before serving.
