French Toast Recipe

Description

This is easily the best french toast recipe out there. This recipe will leave your tastebuds blessed with a crispy outside and a fluffy and tender inside. Whoever graces themselves by eating this french toast that you will make will forever be indebted to you. Plus it's so easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 8 slices of slightly old bread
  • butter
  • syrup + your toppings

Steps

  1. Whisk together eggs, milk, and cinnamon until well blended. Pour into shallow bowl wide enough to place a slice of bread in.
  2. Soak bread slices in egg mixture.
  3. Melt butter in skillet over medium high heat and place bread slices onto skillet. Fry until browned on one side, then flip and brown other side.
  4. Serve French toast with your choice of toppings.
